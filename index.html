ABOUT LAS PINAS CITY

Las Piñas is a highly urbanized city in the National Capital Region of the Philippines. It's located in the southwestern corner of Metro Manila, surrounded by Manila Bay, Bacoor, Muntinlupa, and Parañaque and also Las Piñas is known for its vibrant nightlife and social scene. It's also an affordable place to live, with a relatively low cost of living compared to other major cities in the Philippines.
